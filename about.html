About Gastador

Gastador is a dedicated financial management tool designed specifically for students to navigate their unique economic challenges. The platform focuses on transforming "gastos" (spending) into smart financial habits by providing a clear, visual overview of where your money goes.

Core Purpose

Managing a small allowance while balancing academic costs can be difficult. Gastador helps you maximize every peso by organizing your daily expenses and tracking your growth toward long-term financial security.

Why Use It?

  • Simplicity: Clean interface with light and dark modes.
  • Insights: Educational tips on interest rates & PDIC.
  • Privacy: Data is stored locally on your device.

Key Features for Students

Real-Time Tracking

Set a budget and instantly see remaining balance for meals, commute, or supplies.

Visual Overviews

Interactive charts categorize spending to help identify habits draining your wallet.

Sinking Funds

Plan for laptops or trips by setting dedicated savings goals with deadlines.

EF Builder

Prioritize a safety net for unexpected school fees or personal emergencies.

Asset Management

Track digital bank balances and physical investments in one health overview.

Banking Integration

Recommendations for high-interest banks like Seabank, Maya, and CIMB.
